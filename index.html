Popular Genetics
A life science magazine

Genetics is the branch of biology which study how parents pass their characteristics to their children.

A living cell is as complex as a passenger plane or even a space station. Despite many years of efforts, scientists still have not been able to fully understand all the processes that take place in the cell. I deliberately omit many difficult topics in order to give a clear overall picture. It doesn't pretend to be complete.
