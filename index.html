Learn Genetics: Interactive course

Genetics is the branch of biology which study how parents pass their characteristics to their children. In this brief course, we'll go over the most important aspects of genetics. You will learn what DNA and genes are. How hereditary information is stored in living cells and how mutations arise.

This course is made up of four lessons:

  • Lesson 1: DNA
  • Lesson 2: Genes
  • Lesson 3: Mutations
  • Lesson 4: Inheritance
