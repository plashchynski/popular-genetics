Introduction

This is a magazine about genetics and DNA studies. The site consists of the home page with a list of articles. The articles are intended for beginners and non-professionals interested in this topic. All articles were created by editing information collected from free sources, mainly Wikipedia.

State 3 things that have inspired you when creating your website.

I am fond of computational biology and genetics. I believe that in the near future biotechnology will be actively developed and used to prolong life, treat diseases, and solve social problems. So I've created a magazine with articles for beginners and non-professionals interested in this topic.
